About Us

Altruism and Responsibility

We work not by order and not by status. We work out of altruism, out of inner need to be useful, with a deep awareness of responsibility to those who are defending our country today.

Self-Organization

We are not an officially registered organization and have no formal volunteer status. We don't need thanks or recognition. We need Victory.

We are united by high spiritual values, love for our state, and the desire to live in safety, at home, in a free Ukraine. Each team member has their own work, their own duties, and their own life, but we consciously devote our free time to volunteering â€” because we work from the soul.

Charity Fairs

We organize fairs featuring homemade baked goods, delicacies, souvenirs, and handmade items. We actively conduct fundraising through donation cards.

Hospital Support

We visit military personnel in medical facilities, bring necessary items and moral support. Barbers provide free haircuts for defenders.

Military Artifacts

Flags, chevrons, trophies, and thanks from military personnel for charity sales. Each flag is a sign of trust and deep gratitude for our work.

Business Partnership

We cooperate with indifferent enterprises that have a clear civic position and support Ukrainian military personnel.
